 Electric Vehicles (EV) Data Analysis – Tableau Dashboard
 Project Overview

This project analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) adoption trends using Tableau.
The dashboard explores EV market growth, manufacturer performance, technology improvement, and geographical distribution to understand how electric mobility is evolving.

🎯 Project Objectives

Analyze the total number of electric vehicles

Calculate the average electric range

Compare BEV and PHEV market share

Study EV adoption trends from 2010 onwards

Identify top EV manufacturers and models

Analyze state-wise EV distribution

Examine CAFV eligibility impact

📊 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains information such as:

Model year

Vehicle make and model

Electric vehicle type (BEV / PHEV)

Electric range

State-wise registration data

CAFV eligibility status

Data cleaning and aggregation were performed before visualization.

📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Total Vehicles – Total number of EVs in the dataset

Average Electric Range – Indicates EV technology improvement

Total BEV Vehicles & Percentage – Share of fully electric vehicles

Total PHEV Vehicles & Percentage – Share of hybrid electric vehicles

Key Insights

EV adoption increased significantly after 2018

BEVs dominate the EV market compared to PHEVs

Electric range of BEVs improved steadily over the years

Certain states show higher EV adoption rates

CAFV eligibility programs support EV growth
